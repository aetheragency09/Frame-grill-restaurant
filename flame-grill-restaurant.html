Our Story

Passion Ignited,
Flavour Perfected

Founded in 2010 by Executive Chef Marco Rossetti, Flame Grill was born from a singular vision: to create a sanctuary where the ancient art of fire-cooking meets modern culinary excellence.

We source only the finest ingredients — 28-day aged prime cuts, sustainably caught seafood, locally grown organic produce — and treat each with the respect it deserves. Every flame, every cut, every plate is an act of devotion.
